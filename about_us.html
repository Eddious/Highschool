Mission Statement

Rhodes hall high school is a secondary institution committed to
providing an orderly and safe environment in which the individual will be:

  1. Stimulated to develop creativity, strong morals, values
    and capabilities for lifelong learning.
  2. Empowered to maximize his/her potential to effectively
    function in society and to contribute to furthering its development.
